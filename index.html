关于我

About Me

我是陈蓉杰，福建师范大学计算机与网络空间安全学院网络空间安全专业硕士研究生。我的研究方向聚焦于机器遗忘与隐私保护技术，致力于设计高效可验证的数据删除机制，推动负责任的AI发展。

I am Rongjie Chen, a Master's student in Cybersecurity at Fujian Normal University. My research focuses on Machine Unlearning and Privacy-Preserving Technologies, aiming to design efficient and verifiable data deletion mechanisms for responsible AI.

我相信隐私是基本权利。通过机器遗忘、差分隐私等技术，我们能够在用户数据删除请求、模型更新治理等场景中建立负责任的AI系统。

I believe privacy is a fundamental right. Through machine unlearning and differential privacy, we can build responsible AI systems that honor data deletion requests and model governance.

学术论文

Publications

糖尿病视网膜病变检测基于目标检测算法

Detection of Diabetic Retinopathy Based on Target Detection Algorithm

作者：肖静静, 李万龙, 江梦霞, 陈蓉杰
Authors: Jingjing Xiao, Wanlong Li, Mengxia Jiang, Rongjie Chen
2023年国际网络、通信与智能计算会议 (NCIC)
2023 International Conference on Networks, Communications and Intelligent Computing (NCIC)

摘要：针对糖尿病视网膜病变检测速度慢、精度低的问题，本文开发LadderNet血管分割模型，然后用分割的血管与原始眼底图像背景合成获得血管锐化数据，最后用VGG19网络完成眼底图像分类。血管分割精度达到0.9592，可以实现眼底图像血管的准确分割。

Abstract: This paper develops a LadderNet vascular segmentation model to address slow detection and low accuracy in diabetic retinopathy detection. Vessel segmentation achieves 0.9592 accuracy on the benchmark dataset.
